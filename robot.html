The Robot


2016's Robots are 4 wheeled omnidirection robots featuring the use of light sensor arrays, cameras and IMU's.



Mechanical


Motors

Polulu 25D GearMotor
Key Specs:
 Voltage: 12v
Free Run Speed: 500RPM
Stall Current: 5.6A
Weight: 85g

Solenoid

TRIS10 KickBall Solenoid
Key Specs:
 Resistance: 0.64 Ohms
Weight: 165g
Used in Conjunction with T10 Solenoid Circuit to provide
a full power kick.





Electrical


Main MicroControllers

Micropython V1.1
Key Specs:
 No. of PyBoards: 2
CPU: 168mHz Cortex M4
No. of I/O Pins: 33
No. of Analog Pins: 14
Language: Python 3.4

Motor Controllers

STM L6205N
Key Specs:
 Each motor controller can power 2 motors
No. of Motor Controllers: 2

Light Sensor Array

TEMT6000 PhotoTransistors
Key Specs:
 No. of Photo Transistors: 12
No. of LED's: 12
LED Colour: White
Arrangement: Y-Shape

IR Tracking

TSOP31140
Key Specs:
 No. of TSOPs: 12
Voltage: 1.8v - 5.8v

Camera

CMUcam5 Pixy
Key Specs:
 No. of Cameras: 1
Tracks: Goal
Voltage: 5V

IMU

MPU9250
Key Specs:
 No. of IMUs: 1
Voltage: 3.3v
Feautres:
Gyroscope
Accelerometer
Magnetometer


Robots feature two PCBs. One Main PCB and One Light Array PCB.
PCBs were manufactured by Bittele
