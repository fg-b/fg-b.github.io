The Robot


2017's Robots are 4 wheeled omnidirection robots featuring the use of light sensor arrays, laser, cameras, solenoids and IMU's.



Mechanical


Motors

Maxon DCX19
Key Specs:
 Voltage: 12v
Max RPM (At 12v): 560
Torque (Continuous): 240mNm
Weight: 92g

Solenoid

Takaha Kiko
Key Specs:
 Weight: 105g
Dimensions (mm): 20 x 26 x 37
Travel: 15mm





Electrical


Main MicroControllers

Teensy 3.2
Key Specs:
 No. of Teensies: 3
CPU: 96mHz Cortex M4
No. of I/O Pins: 21
No. of Analog Pins: 21
Language: Arduino

Motor Controllers

LMD18200T
Key Specs:
 Each motor controller can power 1 motor
No. of Motor Controllers: 4

Light Sensor Array

TEMT6000 PhotoTransistors
Key Specs:
 No. of Photo Transistors: 20
No. of LED's: 20
LED Colour: White
Arrangement: Circle

IR Tracking

TSOP31140
Key Specs:
 No. of TSOPs: 12
Voltage: 1.8v - 5.8v

Camera

CMUcam5 Pixy
Key Specs:
 No. of Cameras: 1
Tracks: Goal
Voltage: 5V

IMU

MPU9250
Key Specs:
 No. of IMUs: 1
Voltage: 3.3v
Feautres:
Gyroscope
Accelerometer
Magnetometer


Robots feature two PCBs. One Main PCB and One Light Array PCB.
PCBs were manufactured by PCBWay
